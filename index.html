ATL5D W3B Map

A What3words.com map of ATL5D videos created by ATL5D Discord subscribers.
Visit the @ATLVERSE Youtube channel for the complete list of videos or you can look up the coordinates for individual videos in the Review Interface.


You can contribute to this map over at https://github.com/frog23/TomScottMap. Other feedback, questions and suggestions can be send to maps@frog23.net.
